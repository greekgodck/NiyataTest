About Us

Niyata Infotech Private Limited is a 21st century technology company, formed by a group of industry experts, with the idea of providing intelligent, value added & market relevant technology solutions in the identified niche business verticals as a single customer facing umbrella organization.

THE VISION

Our vision is to be a highly valued technology company in the identified niche business verticals that always meets and regularly surpasses the changing needs and expectations of our customers, end users, employees and potential investors.

THE MISSION

Our mission is to collaborate with the industry leaders and service providers of the identified business verticals to understand their changing product / services needs and to support them with cost effective and efficient technology solutions that can be scaled up for wider usage and mass production.

THE STRATEGY

Our strategy is to keep an open and constant communication with our customers, market experts, end users to identify potential gaps / business opportunities and to diligently invest our time, money & knowledge in effective solutions and their implementations in a time bound and cost effective manner.
